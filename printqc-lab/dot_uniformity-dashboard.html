Interpret the statistics above based on prepress processing theory. How do these numbers translate to physical plate quality?

Compare Global Mean to your 50% target. Is the overall plate over/under exposed? Is the U-Index above 98%?

Look at 'Δ from Global'. Are specific zones printing noticeably stronger (+ve) or weaker (-ve)? What could cause this mechanically?
